About Us

A Team of Dedicated Dentist

Welcome to SHREE KALYAN DENTAL CARE in RAIPUR, where Dr. Singh and our specialists provide pain-free, comprehensive dental services for all ages, ensuring top-quality care and healthy smiles.

Dr. Shailja Singh

Dr. Shailja Singh is a skilled dental professional with a strong educational foundation and expertise in the field. She holds a Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) from Hitkarini Dental College, Jabalpur, Madhya Pradesh.

Her experience includes working at Vidya Dental Care and My Smile Dental Care in Samta Colony, Raipur. She is also the proud owner of Shree Kalyan Dental Care. Her areas of expertise lie in general and cosmetic dentistry, where she excels in providing exceptional care to her patients.

She is a proud member of the Indian Dental Association (IDA), Raipur Branch. Her dedication to the field and professional affiliation make her a trusted practitioner in the world of dentistry.

Dr. Shreya Shukla

Dr. Shreya Shukla is a highly qualified dental professional with a remarkable academic journey and extensive expertise. She earned her Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) from Rungta College of Dental Sciences & Research, Bhilai, and completed her clinical internship at GDC, Raipur, Chattisgarh. Dr. Shukla further excelled by attaining a Master of Dental Surgery (MDS) from Rungta College of Dental Sciences & Research.

Her areas of expertise are diverse and encompass various aspects of oral and maxillofacial surgery. Dr. Shukla's interests lie in maxillofacial trauma, surgical extractions, pathological lesions, oncosurgery, cystic lesions, local flaps, incision & drainage, patient management protocol, and suturing in head & surgery.

She is an esteemed member of professional dental organizations, including the Association of Oral & Maxillofacial Surgeons of India and the Indian Dental Association (IDA), Raipur, CG. Dr. Shukla's extensive educational background and clinical experience make her a trusted and skilled practitioner in the field of oral and maxillofacial surgery.

Dr. Tanushree Khandelwal

She is an esteemed member of professional dental organizations, including the Chattisgarh State Dental Council, the Indian Orthodontic Society, and the Indian Dental Association (IDA). Her extensive knowledge and dedication make her a trusted name in the field of dentistry.
